// how_it_works
01

Connect your stack

Point forager-sre at your telemetry and repos — Datadog, Prometheus, logs, Kubernetes, GitHub. Read-only by default, so it never touches prod uninvited.

02

Bring your own model

One adapter, any LLM. Hosted frontier models, an open-weights model on your GPUs, or a fully local runtime — swap with a single env var.

03

Investigate on autopilot

The agent triages every alert, correlates signals across services, and proposes a fix — each claim cited back to the exact metric, log line, or deploy.

// demo

From zero to on-call in four commands.

No SaaS lock-in, no agent framework to learn. Install the binary, point it at your stack, choose a model, and let it watch your alerts.

✓ Single static binary — no Python env, no daemon zoo.
✓ Every conclusion cites the metric, log, or deploy behind it.
✓ Your data and your model — runs fully air-gapped if you need.
zsh — ~/svc
# 1 · install the binary
$ curl -fsSL forager-sre.dev/install | sh
# 2 · connect your stack (read-only)
$ forager init --provider datadog --repo .
# 3 · bring any model
$ export FORAGER_MODEL=claude-sonnet # or gpt-4o, llama3, local
# 4 · go on-call
$ forager watch
◇ watching 14 services · model=claude-sonnet
◇ ready — listening for alerts
